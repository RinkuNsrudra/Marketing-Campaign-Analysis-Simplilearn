**Marketing Campaign Analysis using Python**

**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing marketing campaign data to understand the factors influencing customer acquisition and campaign effectiveness. Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Hypothesis Testing, the project examines customer demographics, purchasing behavior, sales channels, and promotional campaign performance.

The analysis is based on the Marketing Mix framework, which emphasizes the four key elements of marketing: People, Product, Place, and Promotion. By investigating these dimensions, the project provides actionable insights that can help businesses optimize marketing strategies and improve customer engagement.

**Business Problem**
Marketing campaigns require significant investment, making it essential to understand which factors drive customer acquisition and purchasing decisions. Organizations need data-driven insights to identify target customers, optimize promotional activities, and maximize return on investment (ROI).

As a Data Scientist, the objective is to analyze customer and campaign data to uncover patterns, validate assumptions through statistical testing, and generate recommendations that support effective marketing decisions.

**Project Objectives**
Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on customer and marketing campaign data.

Analyze customer demographics and spending behavior.

Understand the impact of marketing campaigns on customer acquisition.

Evaluate the effectiveness of different sales channels.

Conduct hypothesis testing to validate business assumptions.

Generate actionable business insights and recommendations.
